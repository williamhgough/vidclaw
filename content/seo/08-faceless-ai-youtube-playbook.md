# Faceless AI YouTube Playbook for Mimir Works

## Goal
Ship one repeatable YouTube system that turns research, scripts, and edits into a weekly upload without requiring on-camera presence.

## Best format
- 6 to 10 minute videos
- Screen-recorded walkthroughs
- AI voiceover or clean text-to-speech
- Simple motion graphics, captions, and cursor emphasis
- One clear promise per video

## Content angles
1. AI tool tutorials for builders
2. Workflow automations that save time
3. Side-project teardown videos
4. AI agency process breakdowns
5. SaaS and open-source explainers

## Repeatable workflow
1. Pick one problem with search intent.
2. Write a 7-point outline.
3. Record screen demo first.
4. Generate voiceover from the outline.
5. Add captions, title cards, and callouts.
6. Export shorts from the best 2 clips.
7. Publish longform on YouTube, then distribute clips to X, LinkedIn, and Reddit.

## Tool stack
- Research: YouTube search, Google search, and competitor channels
- Script: Markdown in vault, then polish
- Voice: TTS or clone only if quality is good enough
- Edit: CapCut, Descript, or ffmpeg-based assembly
- Thumbnails: simple high-contrast text, one subject, one promise

## Distribution loop
- Longform video on YouTube
- 2 to 3 shorts clipped from the same recording
- One blog post summary for SEO
- One social post with the strongest takeaway

## Execution checklist
- [ ] Pick one keyword with clear intent and low competition
- [ ] Draft the outline in 7 beats
- [ ] Record the screen demo before polishing script
- [ ] Generate voiceover after the demo is locked
- [ ] Export 2 shorts and 1 blog summary from the same asset
- [ ] Schedule the publish + distribution batch in one sitting
